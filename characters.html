Main characters:

Below are a few of the lore heavy characters

Princess Zelda is the Hylian Princess of Hyrule, she holds the Triforce of Wisdom. She is trapped in Hyrule Castle waiting for Link to rescue her.

Link is the Hero choosen by the goddess Hylia, he is Zelda's personal Knight. He holds the Triforce of Courage.

Urbosa is the Gerudo Champion, she is the Gerudo chief chosen to command the Divine Beast Vah Naboris.

Revali is the Rito Champion, he is the chosen archer to command the Divine Beast Vah Medoh.

Daruk is the Goron Champion, he is chosen to command the Divine Beast Vah Rudania.

Mipha is the Zora Champion, she is chosen to command the Divine Beast Vah Ruta.

Quick Facts Information
Princess Zelda: Princess of Hyrule Zelda initially resents Link because his natural talenst as a knight reminded her of her own failures. She eventually grows to love and trust him deeply.
Link: The Hero Unlike popular belief Link is not mute, according to Zelda's Diary which you can access in her personal tower in the castle, Link rarely speaks because he feels the weight of his responsibilites and must be strong. This causes him to stay silent and bear burdens alone.
Mipha: The Zora Champion Mipha was childhood friends with Link, she fell in love with him even. In Zora culture when one wants to wed someone they gift them custom made armour set as a proposal. Mipha died before she could gift link the set. The set itself can be found through certain quests during the gameplay.
Durak: The Goron Champion Despite his size and bravery, Daruk has an intense fear of dogs. This is a lingering effect from his childhood where a dog chased him. He is very embarrassed by this weakness and panics when confronted.
Urbosa: The Gerudo Champion Urbosa is known for her immense skill and speed, described as looking like a "Beautiful Dance" in battle. She has the ability to summon lighting at the snap of her fingers. This power is aquired after you defeat Vah Naboris in BOTW.
Revali: The Rito Champion Revali is very hostile towards Link, the reason for this stems from jealousy over Link being appointed as Princess Zelda's KNight and recieving the Master Sword as he sees himself to be the superior warrior.
