Locations and Divine Beasts:

Below are some locations and there biggest threats

The Map of Hyrule. You unlock each section by travelling to all the towers around the map. Each one more or less challenging then your last.

Gerudo Town, Located in the Gerudo Desert. The town itself is Females only, Men are not allowed inside. To gain access Link has to dress in female attire in order to get past the guards.

Divine Beast Vah Naboris, This magnificent camel like mechanical construct roams the Gerudo desert causing sandstorms and people going missing. Once defeated you gain Urbosa,s Fury.

Death Mountain is a Dangerous and hot place, the only residence are Gorons as their skin can handle the molten lava heat. To get to the town you must follow the path around the moutain, in order to do this you need fireproof potions or food until you can aquire fireproof armour in the city itself.

Divine Beast Vah Rudania, This adorable little lizard construct is a personal favorite of mine. Vah Rudania has been causing a stir on the mountain. Causing lava rocks to fall down on the people of Goron city as well as making the lava rise. Once defeated you gain Daruk's Protection.

Zora's Domain is home to the Zora. It is located in the Lanayru region of the map You must travel up winding roads during the pouring rain while risking being attacked by lightning wielding weapons.

Divine Beast Vah Ruta, This Elphant looking construct is constantly raining down on Zora's Domain, slowly flooding it. Once defeated you gain Miphas Grace.

Rito Village is located in the northwest region of hyrule. It is the home of the Rito. You must travel up winding hills and then through freezing cold environments before reaching Rito village. You will need cold resistance potions or food in order to make it there.

Divine Beast Vah Medoh, This bird looking construct is flying high above Rito village preventing any bird from flying to high. Once defeated you gain Revali's Gale.
