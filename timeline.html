Timeline murals:

The following murals are seen in Tears of the Kingdom, the game that "follows" Breath of the wild.

In the past the Zonai who lived in the sky above hyrule, saw the threat of Ganon and decided to form a union between the Zonai king Raruru and the Hylian Queen Sonia

After being rejected by the Zonai king, Ganon steals the Triforce of power and therefore becomes corrupted by the mix of his greed and it's overwhelming power.

The Zonai hold powerful stones that grant certain magic to their holders.

The alliance between the Zonai and the Hylians was not enough to defeat ganon, after the death of his wife Sonia, Raruru gives his life to trap Ganon under Hyrule Castle for 1000 years. Breath of the wild takes place after his escape in the future.
